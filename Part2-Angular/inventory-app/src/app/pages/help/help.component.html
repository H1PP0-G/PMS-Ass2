Help & FAQ

Q: How do I add a new item?

A: Go to 'Manage Items', fill in all fields (ID must be unique), and click 'Add Item'.

Q: Why is my data gone after refresh?

A: This app is designed for session interaction only. It does not use a persistent database, as per security requirements for this session.

Q: How can I delete an item?

A: Enter the exact 'Item Name' in the manage page and click 'Delete by Name'. You will be asked for confirmation.
